# ED452C Journal Article Review

## Requirements

- Find one empirical journal article on Social-Emotional Learning (SEL) or Positive Behavior Intervention and Supports (PBIS).
- Write a one-page reflection.
- Include the article title, authors, journal name, and APA citation.
- Summarize the key information from the article.
- Explain what was learned from reading the article.

## Rubric Targets

- Article must be empirical and focused on SEL or PBIS.
- APA citation must be correct.
- Summary must be complete enough for the reader to understand the article.
- Learned knowledge must be explained in depth.

## Selected Article Candidate

Brackett, M. A., Rivers, S. E., Reyes, M. R., and Salovey, P. (2012). Enhancing academic performance and social and emotional competence with the RULER Feeling Words Curriculum. Learning and Individual Differences, 22(2), 218-224.

## Source File Needed

Download the article PDF through Rush Rhees Library or ScienceDirect and save it in this directory as:

brackett_rivers_reyes_salovey_2012_ruler.pdf

Once the PDF is uploaded, use it as the direct source for the reflection.
