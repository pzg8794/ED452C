

**ED452C Journal Article Review (25% of overall grade)**

**Journal Article Review Directions**

Using the library databases, find an empirical journal article on Social-Emotional Learning (SEL) or Positive Behavior Intervention and Supports (PBIS). Read the article and then write a one-page reflection. In your reflection, you need to state the title of the article, the authors, and the journal it came from (cite the article in APA). Your reflection needs to include a summary of the key information from the article as well as what you learned from the reading. Please refer to the rubric on Blackboard. You will submit your article in Blackboard, and it can be turned in at any point during the course by the due date. Here is the link to the Rush Rhees Library Database: https://www.library.rochester.edu/spaces/rush-rhees
