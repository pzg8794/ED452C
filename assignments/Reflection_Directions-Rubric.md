**ED452C Reflection (25% of overall grade)**

**Reflection Directions**

Personal and professional reflection are key components of the learning process. For this assignment, you will write a two to three-page reflection on what you have gained from taking this course. Please refer to the rubric on Blackboard. You will submit your reflection in Blackboard as a word document by the due date. You should guide your paper around the following questions:

* What topics in this course do you feel you had a good understanding of?  
* What topics do you want to learn more about?  
* How are you going to pursue further learning?

| *Criterion* | *Level* | *Meaning* |
| :---- | :---: | ----- |

|  | 2 | The writer somewhat lists topics from the course that they now have a good understanding of. |
